Website Proposal

Zungeni Transport Services

Business Overview

Zungeni Transport Services is a Cape Town-based private transport company owned and operated by Sinethemba Praise Silinda, also known as Willie. The business focuses on providing safe, reliable, affordable, and comfortable transportation services to individuals, students, tourists, and professionals.

The company offers private charter services, airport transfers, local transport, long-distance trips, tours, and private chauffeuring services. Zungeni Transport Services operates mainly throughout the Western Cape while also providing transport services to Johannesburg and Mpumalanga, including tours around the Kruger National Park.

The business allows customers to make bookings directly through WhatsApp and phone enquiries, creating a more personalised and convenient customer experience.

Mission Statement

To provide safe, reliable, affordable, and comfortable transport services while ensuring excellent customer satisfaction at all times.

⸻

Vision Statement

To become one of the most trusted and recognised private transport brands in South Africa through professionalism, reliability, and quality service.

Target Audience

The business mainly targets:

* Students
* Working professionals
* Tourists
* Local residents
* Families requiring private transport
* Individuals travelling long distances

⸻

Website Goals and Objectives

Main Goal

The primary goal of the website is to establish a professional online presence for Zungeni Transport Services and make it easier for customers to access transport services and booking information.

Objectives

The website aims to:

* Provide detailed information about transport services
* Allow customers to make enquiries and bookings
* Promote the business professionally online
* Increase customer trust and credibility
* Showcase available vehicles and transport experiences
* Improve communication between clients and the business

⸻

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The success of the website will be measured using the following KPIs:

* Number of customer enquiries received
* Increase in transport bookings
* Growth in repeat customers
* Website traffic and visitor engagement
* Customer feedback and satisfaction
* WhatsApp booking activity
