Zungeni Transport Services 
Zungeni Transport Services is a Cape Town-based private transport service owned and 
operated by Snethemba Praise Silinda. The business focuses on providing safe, reliable, 
and affordable transportation for individuals, students, and professionals. 
The service operates through ride-hailing platforms such as Uber and Bolt, while also 
offering direct bookings for clients who prefer a more personalised transport experience. 
Mission: 
To provide safe, reliable, and accessible transport services to all clients. 
Vision: 
To become a trusted and recognised private transport brand in Cape Town. 
Target Audience: 
* Students 
* Working professionals 
* Tourists 
* Local residents needing reliable transport
